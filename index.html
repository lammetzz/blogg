About us

Welcome to "Discover Växjö": Your Guide to Tourist Life in Sweden!

Sweden is known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage and welcoming atmosphere. If you've ever dreamed of being a tourist in this beautiful country, you've come to the right place. Welcome to our blog, where we take you on an exciting journey through one of Sweden's most charming cities - Växjö.

Växjö, a gem in Småland, offers a unique combination of modernity and tradition. Here you will find picturesque lakes, verdant forests, and a vibrant city center. Whether you are an avid adventurer, a history buff or just a curious traveler, Växjö has something to offer you. Through this blog, we will guide you through the best places to visit, the tastiest dishes to taste, and the most memorable experiences this friendly city has to offer.

So, why not take a few minutes, relax and let us take you into the wonderful world of Växjö, Sweden? Whether you are a local resident who wants to see your city with new eyes or a foreign visitor who dreams of a Scandinavian adventure, you are most welcome to join us on this journey through Växjö's charming streets and scenic surroundings.

We promise you a memorable experience that will awaken your curiosity and desire to explore Sweden even more. Join us as we bring you the best tips, stories and photos from our discovery trip in Växjö, Sweden. Let's discover together the hidden treasure of this beautiful city!
